Bibimbap

Delicious Bibimbap!

Something about how Bibimbap is so delicious, healthy and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Variety of veggies and greens
  • Meat, if you want
  • Fried egg
  • Gochujang

How to make it

  1. Add rice to a big bowl.
  2. Spread meat, veggies and greens over the rice.
  3. Top off with a fried egg and Gochujang.
  4. Eat!

See? I told you it was easy! Bon appetit!
