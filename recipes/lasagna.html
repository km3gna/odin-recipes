THE Lasagna

A divine combination of minced meat between herbed lasagna pasta sheets.

This lasagna recipe might take some time to prepare but, when you pull the end result out of the oven, you will know it was totally worth it.

THE Ingredients

  • 9 Lasagna sheets
  • 1/2 kg chicken or minced meat
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, pureed or from a can
  • 4 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ... you get the idea ...

How to...

Prepare the pasta sheets

  1. Bring salted water to boil and put in pasta sheets.
  2. Cool to al dente (8-10 minutes). Remove and drain.
  3. Drizzle a little olive oil over them so they don't stick.

Prepare the sauce

  1. In a large frying pan, add olive oil and saute onion until transclucent.
  2. Add garlic and minced meat (or chicken). Let it brown and then cover and cook over low heat for ten minutes.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, pasta sauce, herbs, etc and cook on low until there is a thick, mushy consistency.
  4. Add freshly torn basil leaves.
  5. In a bowl, beat eggs lightly and add cheeses.

Assemble and bake

  1. In a lasagna pan, ladle 1/3 of sauce to cover the bottom.
  2. Place 3 pasta sheets over the sauce, covering the bottom.
  3. Add 1/3 egg-cheese mixture.
  4. Repeat utnil there are 3 layers.
  5. Bake at 180 degrees (C? F?) for 1 hour.
  6. Let it sit for 10 minutes and then dig in!

Bon Appetit!
